Tula Ram Paudel







About Me

Tula got his M.Sc. in Physics, specifically the Central Department of Physics, at Tribhuvan University, Nepal in 2001. Next, he got his Post-M.Sc. Condensed Matter Physics Diploma from The International Center for Theoretical Physics, Italy in 2004. Lastly, he got his PhD in Condensed Matter Physics from Case Western Reserve University, Ohio in 2009. At SDSM&T, He is an Assistant Professer in the Department of Physics. He has taught many courses like Gereral Physics 1, Gereral Physics 2, and Introductory Quantum Mechanics.
